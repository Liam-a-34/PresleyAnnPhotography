About Presley Ann

Capturing precious moments is my favorite thing to do! I have a passion working the camera in any environment. Having good quality pictures means more than just a social media post... they hold memories that last a lifetime.

Hi I'm Presley. I'm so glad you're here!

I am a photographer born and raised in Middle Tennessee. I have always combined my love for photography with my love for agriculture and livestock. I grew up exhibiting livestock through 4H and was a leader in my high school's FFA chapter. I now attend Middle Tennessee State University where I am continuing to study agriculture.

My love for photography is rooted in capturing memories both in and out of the show ring that families can cherish forever.

I believe that we are all God's masterpiece and I would love to capture a piece of your masterpiece.
